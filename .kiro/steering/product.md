# Product Overview

This repository contains multiple AI-powered business automation projects focused on e-commerce and workflow orchestration.

## Primary Products

### AI Workflow Architect
A full-stack multi-agent AI orchestration platform that enables teams to coordinate multiple AI providers (OpenAI, Anthropic, xAI, Perplexity, Google Gemini) with cost governance, secure credential storage, and decision tracing.

**Key Features:**
- Multi-provider AI orchestration with automatic fallback
- Real-time budget tracking and enforcement
- Encrypted credential vault (AES-256-GCM)
- Decision tracing with approval workflows
- Centralized memory system with keyword search

### Gumroad Automation Demo
AI-powered browser automation for Gumroad product publishing using Skyvern + FastAPI for automating web forms and workflows.

**Key Features:**
- Automated product creation and publishing
- Form filling and validation
- Sales webhook integration
- Email notification systems
- Customer onboarding automation

## Business Goals

**Mission:** Build and launch profitable e-commerce automation tools and apps to support sole proprietorship business across multiple platforms.

**Target Revenue:** Scale from $1,000/month (Q1) to $30,000/month (Q4) through digital products, app licenses, and SaaS subscriptions.

**Target Markets:**
- Shopify store owners needing mobile management
- Amazon FBA/FBM sellers
- Product sourcing entrepreneurs
- E-commerce entrepreneurs without design skills
- Multi-channel sellers
